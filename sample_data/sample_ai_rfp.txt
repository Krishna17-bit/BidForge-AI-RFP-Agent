ACME Manufacturing Group - Request for Proposal
Project: AI Document Intelligence and Operations Automation Pilot

ACME is seeking a technology partner to design and implement a 6-week pilot for an AI assistant that helps operations and procurement teams search internal SOPs, maintenance manuals, supplier contracts, and incident reports. The solution must support document upload, source-grounded answers, audit logs, and a human approval step before any outbound email or workflow update.

Scope of work:
1. The vendor shall build a secure web interface where operations users can upload PDF, DOCX, TXT, and CSV files.
2. The system must extract key information from supplier contracts, maintenance manuals, and SOP documents.
3. The assistant should answer questions with citations to source documents and should say when evidence is missing.
4. The vendor must include a risk review workflow for unsupported claims, missing evidence, and ambiguous user requests.
5. The system should classify document type and route the request to the correct workflow.
6. The solution must include an admin-facing audit log of user questions, retrieved sources, generated answers, and approval decisions.
7. The vendor should include a dashboard summarizing document coverage, common risks, and unresolved questions.
8. The solution should integrate with Google Drive or SharePoint in a later phase, but the pilot may use local upload.
9. The vendor must provide deployment documentation and a handover session.
10. The vendor should complete the pilot within 6 weeks.
11. Security requirements include no training on client data, environment-based secrets, role-based access in the production phase, and clear data retention assumptions.
12. The proposal should include milestones, assumptions, key risks, and a rough team structure.

Evaluation criteria:
- Relevant experience with RAG, document AI, workflow automation, and Python/FastAPI or similar backend systems.
- Quality of implementation approach and architecture.
- Evidence of ability to handle hallucination risk and auditability.
- Practical timeline and communication process.
- Clarity of proposal and assumptions.

Questions for vendor:
- What is your recommended pilot architecture?
- How will you prevent unsupported answers?
- What information do you need from ACME before kickoff?
- How would you scale the pilot into production?
